Local knowledge. Mountain heart.

Based in Sibiu, we know the Carpathians intimately — the ridges, the valleys, and the small tracks that matter.

The Idea

Our 2022 Făgăraș Ridge trip, along with many other family trips, inspired us to help hikers explore the Carpathians with less load and more freedom. We believe the mountains should be enjoyed — not suffered through under the weight of the gear needed at the end of the day. It is comforting to know that after a long day of hiking, you can relax in your own tent, with a hot meal and a beverage of choice that you did not have to carry the entire way.

Our Mission

LightPack Adventures exists to make multi-day mountain travel more accessible and joyful. We combine local route knowledge, reliable logistics, and a strict leave-no-trace policy to support hikers of all levels.

How It Works

More than a service — it’s a partnership in the wild.

Every trip we support is unique. There’s no one-size-fits-all price list, because each route, drop-off point, and timing depends on your adventure’s exact needs. Before we set foot (or wheel) on the trail, we’ll talk through:

Your route

We’ll help identify the best drop-off and pick-up points in the Carpathians or wherever your adventure takes you.

Your gear

We’ll confirm what you need carried, and how to pack it for the trail.

Your timing

We’ll align on exact delivery and pick-up windows so you can rely on us, even deep in the mountains.

Pack Light

You carry essentials, needed for the day; we bring the rest.

Adventure Fully

Arrive to find gear, food, and comfort waiting for you, just as we planned.

Gear Drops & Resupply

We bring gear like tents, sleeping bags, food, water at agreed checkpoints.

Porter Support

Experienced mountain porters carry heavy loads across trails to ensure that you have what you need when you need it.

On and Off-road Logistics

We take care of all logistics to ensure that everything you need is available when we agree. Our team will work with you to plan and execute your logistics needs, from transportation to storage, to ensure that you have everything you need to succeed.

Overnight Support

If timing requires, our staff can wait overnight and return to collect equipment.

We know that your journey depends on having the right gear in the right place at the right time. That’s why building trust with every client is critical. You need to be certain that when you arrive, we’ll be there — no excuses.

Custom Pricing — Transparent Components

Every trip is custom. Pricing depends on: number of days, elevation & distance to drop points, load weight, number of porters required, vehicle usage, and overnight staffing.

A 3-day supported hiking adventure | Published January 2025

The Fagaras Mountains offer some of Romania's most spectacular ridge walking, but the logistics of carrying multiple days of gear can turn an incredible adventure into an exhausting burden. This is where LightPack Adventures transforms your experience - allowing you to focus on the breathtaking views and challenging terrain while we handle your heavy equipment.

📅 Timing & Coordination

Pre-Trip Planning (2 weeks before)

  • Weather Assessment: We monitor conditions and suggest optimal dates
  • Gear Check: Review your equipment list and supplement what's needed
  • Route Briefing: Detailed discussion of the path, waypoints, and emergency procedures
  • Drop-off Time: Typically 7:00 AM at Balea Lac for optimal hiking conditions

Essential Gear Discussion

What you carry daily:

  • Lightweight day pack (20-30L)
  • Water (2-3L), food, and snacks of your choice
  • Weather protection (rain gear, extra layer)
  • Navigation tools (map, GPS device, phone) and emergency whistle
  • First aid basics and personal medication

What we transport for you (to be discussed, but as an example):

  • Tent, sleeping bag, and sleeping pad
  • Cooking equipment and stove fuel
  • Main meals and cooking supplies
  • Extra clothing and toiletries
  • Emergency shelter and repair kit

🏔️ The Adventure Unfolds

Day 1: Balea Lac Drop-off

Your adventure begins at 7:00 AM when we meet you at the agreed location and drive to Balea Lac (2,034m). This iconic starting point offers immediate immersion into the high alpine environment. After a final gear check and route briefing, you'll begin your hike along the main Fagaras ridge.

The route starts with an introduction to the most technical part of the ridge towards the second biggest peak in Romania Negoiu Peak (2,535m). It continues westward along the spectacular ridge line. The terrain is challenging but well-marked (ridge, red line), with stunning views of the Olt Valley to the north and the Arges Valley to the south.

Trust Point: As you approach Caltun Lake in the afternoon, your tent, sleeping system, and dinner supplies are already waiting in a weatherproof cache. We've been there ahead of you, ensuring everything is secure and ready.

Day 2: The Heart of the western Ridge

While you sleep comfortably at Caltun Lake, we quietly collect your previous day's cache and begin preparing the next stage. You wake refreshed, pack your day essentials, and continue west along the ridge toward Avrig Lake.

Today's route passes several significant peaks including Negoiu, Serbota, Scara and the well-known Custura Saratii, with optional detours if you see fit. The ridge walking is spectacular, with 360-degree mountain views and diverse alpine flora.

Trust Point: At Avrig Lake, your fresh gear cache awaits - clean clothes, restocked food supplies, and your familiar camp setup. We maintain the highest standards of reliability because we understand that in the mountains, trust isn't just about service - it's about safety.

Day 3: Descent to Turnu Rosu

Your final day involves the descent from the high ridge to Turnu Rosu Monastery. Before this, Budislavu and Suru peaks great you with stunning views. This is typically the most straightforward day in terms of navigation and difficulty, following well-established trails down from the alpine meadows, through diverse forest zones.

The descent offers a completely different perspective - from alpine lakes and rocky peaks to ancient forests and eventually the historic monastery setting. It's a perfect conclusion that showcases the incredible diversity of the Carpathian landscape.

Trust Point: We're waiting at Turnu Rosu Monastery at the agreed time (typically in the afternoon). Your transport back to Sibiu is ready, along with cold drinks and a debrief of your incredible journey.

Every LightPack adventure is built on mutual trust. You trust us to be where we say we'll be, when we say we'll be there, with the gear you need to safely complete your journey. We trust you to be prepared, responsible, and honest about your capabilities and needs.

This partnership allows both of us to focus on what we do best: you experience the incredible freedom of ridge walking without heavy packs, while we ensure seamless logistics that make your adventure possible.

With LightPack support, you carry just 5-8kg daily while still experiencing the full adventure and challenge of the Fagaras ridge. This isn't about making the mountains easier - it's about making them accessible and enjoyable while maintaining the authentic mountain experience.

🎒 How Our Gear Support Works

Our comprehensive gear support system is designed to make your multi-day cycling adventure comfortable and worry-free:

📦 Pre-Adventure Preparation

Before your departure, you provide us with your gear bags containing everything you need for each segment of the journey. We organize and prepare these bags for strategic placement along your route.

🚗 Strategic Gear Drops

We place your gear bags at the official Via Transilvanica stops in each village according to the recommended route. Your bags will be waiting for you when you arrive, containing fresh clothes, camping equipment, food supplies, or any other essentials you've packed.

🔄 Bag Exchange Service

After using your gear for the day, you have the option to leave a bag behind for pickup. We'll collect these bags and return them to you at the end of your journey in Bucova, ensuring you don't accumulate unnecessary weight.

🗺️ Customer-Selected Stops

You choose your preferred stops from the official Via Transilvanica recommended locations. Whether you want to overnight in historic Alba-Iulia or prefer the mountain village atmosphere of Nucșoara, we adapt our service to your chosen itinerary.

🏛️ Daily Highlights

Day 1: Micăsasa to Blaj (≈40km)

Begin your journey with a morning drop-off in Micăsasa, a charming village that serves as your gateway to the Terra Dacica route. The ride to Blaj takes you through rolling hills and traditional Romanian countryside. Blaj awaits with its rich cultural heritage and your first gear drop.

Day 2: Blaj to Alba-Iulia (≈45km)

Cycle towards Alba-Iulia, one of Romania's most historically significant cities. The route offers beautiful landscapes and culminates in exploring the impressive Alba Carolina Citadel. Your gear awaits at the official Via Transilvanica stop.

Day 3: Alba-Iulia to Pianul de Sus (≈40km)

Leave the urban environment behind as you head towards the mountains. Pianul de Sus offers a peaceful mountain village atmosphere and serves as your base for exploring the higher elevations of the region.

Day 4: Pianul de Sus to Ciungu Mare (≈35km)

Navigate through increasingly mountainous terrain as you approach the heartland of ancient Dacia. The landscape becomes more dramatic with each pedal stroke, building anticipation for the archaeological wonders ahead.

Day 5: Ciungu Mare to Sarmizegetusa Regia (≈45km)

The highlight of your journey: reach Sarmizegetusa Regia, the ancient capital of Dacia. This UNESCO World Heritage site offers an incredible glimpse into pre-Roman civilization. Your gear drop here includes everything needed for exploring this magnificent archaeological site.

Day 6: Sarmizegetusa Regia to Nucșoara (≈40km)

Continue through the Orăștie Mountains to reach Nucșoara, a village with a complex history and stunning mountain setting. The terrain remains challenging but rewarding, with spectacular views throughout.

Day 7: Nucșoara to Bucova (≈45km)

Your final day concludes this epic journey in Bucova, where we'll be waiting with your pickup service. Celebrate completing the Terra Dacica route while we handle the logistics of getting you and your gear home.

🎒 Comprehensive Gear Support Service

Our mountain pass cycling support service is designed to make these challenging high-altitude routes accessible and enjoyable, allowing you to focus on the incredible cycling experience:

🚗 Flexible Start Options

Begin your adventure with drop-off in Cărtișoara for the classic route, or optionally start from Sibiu if you prefer a longer warm-up approach to the Transfăgărășan. We adapt to your preferences and fitness level.

🏨 Strategic Overnight Support

Your gear for the night and supplies for day two will be waiting for you at your chosen drop-off point in Curtea de Argeș. No need to carry camping equipment or extra clothing up and over the Transfăgărășan pass.

🔄 Optional Bag Exchange

After your overnight stay, leave behind any used gear or equipment you no longer need. We'll pick up these bags from the same location and return them to you at the end of your journey, keeping your load light for the Transalpina challenge.

🎯 Multiple Finish Options

Choose your preferred finish point: Sugag for the classic route end, Jina for a traditional mountain village experience, or continue all the way to Sibiu if you want to complete a full circuit adventure.

⛰️ Day-by-Day Adventure

Day 1: Cărtișoara to Curtea de Argeș via Transfăgărășan (~90km)

The Legendary Climb: Begin your epic journey with one of Romania's most famous roads. The Transfăgărășan will challenge you with its relentless switchbacks as you climb over 2000 meters in altitude. The views become more spectacular with every turn.

The Thrilling Descent: After conquering the pass, enjoy the exhilarating descent past the impressive Vidraru Dam. This engineering marvel, set against the dramatic mountain backdrop, provides perfect photo opportunities and a well-deserved break.

Evening Rest: Arrive in Curtea de Argeș where your overnight gear and tomorrow's supplies await you. Rest and recover while anticipating tomorrow's Transalpina challenge.

Day 2: Curtea de Argeș to Sugag via Transalpina (~110km)

Romania's Highest Pass: Take on the Transalpina, Romania's highest mountain pass at 2145 meters. Known as the "King's Road," this challenging climb will test your limits but reward you with some of Europe's most stunning alpine scenery.

Epic Views and Descents: The hard-earned climb leads to unforgettable panoramic views from the top. Your reward is the spectacular descent past Oașa Dam, with breathtaking mountain vistas that make every pedal stroke of the climb worthwhile.

Flexible Finish: Complete your adventure in Sugag, or continue to Jina for a traditional mountain village experience, or push on to Sibiu to complete the full circuit.
